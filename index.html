How it works

TempLight uses as thermal camera to detect your presence in a room. When you leave the room, it...

  • Turns off the light behind you
  • Turns the heating down to a pre-set temperature
  • Turns off select eletrical devices (e.g. TV, oven, etc.)
  • Gets into standby mode until motion sensors turn TempLight back on
